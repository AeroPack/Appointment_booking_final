About Dr. Jenkins

Dr. Sarah Jenkins is a board-certified cardiologist dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based cardiovascular care. She earned her medical degree from Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine and completed her fellowship at the Mayo Clinic.

Her approach integrates the latest diagnostic technologies with personalized lifestyle interventions, ensuring that each patient receives a comprehensive heart health strategy. She is a frequent contributor to international cardiac journals and lead researcher on modern heart failure prevention.
